Classic Chocolate Layer Cake

(4.5 — 128 reviews)

Indulge in our signature Classic Chocolate Layer Cake — three layers of rich, moist chocolate sponge enveloped in velvety dark chocolate ganache. Made with premium Belgian cocoa and real vanilla, this cake is a chocolate lover's dream. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or any moment that deserves something extraordinary.

Category: Chocolate Cakes

Availability: In Stock

Delivery: Same-day for orders before 2 PM

Our Classic Chocolate Layer Cake is the crown jewel of Sweet Crumbs Bakery. Each cake is handcrafted from scratch using time-honored recipes perfected over a decade. We use only the finest Belgian cocoa, farm-fresh eggs, and real Madagascar vanilla to create a cake that's rich, moist, and utterly decadent.

The three layers of chocolate sponge are filled and frosted with our signature dark chocolate ganache — a silky-smooth blend of premium dark chocolate and heavy cream. The exterior is finished with a beautiful ganache drip and chocolate shavings for a stunning presentation.

Serving Size: This cake serves 10-12 people generously.
Storage: Store in a cool, dry place. Best enjoyed within 3 days of purchase. Can be refrigerated for up to 5 days.
